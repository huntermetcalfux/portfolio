User Experience (UX) Design

noun. {yoo-zer \ ik-ˈspir-ē-ən(t)s \ dihy-n}

1. Any interaction with a product or service is referred to as the user experience or UX. Every aspect that defines this experience is considered in UX design, as well as the user's feelings and how simple it is for them to complete their intended activities. 2. This includes everything from how a tangible item feels in your hands to how easy the virtual checkout process is. UX design strives to provide users with simple, practical, and enjoyable experiences.
